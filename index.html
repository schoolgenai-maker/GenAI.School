About the School

Immersive, code-first, and research-driven — learn by doing.

Welcome to the International School on Emerging Technologies of Generative AI — a high-intensity bootcamp where slides are minimal and real-world coding is everything. Over three days, participants will dive deep into Large Language Models, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, and autonomous AI Agents.

  • Dates: May 4–6, 2026 (5h sessions/day · 9:00 – 14:00)
  • Location: ENET'Com, University of Sfax, Tunisia (onsite only)
  • Format: Hands-on workshops, theory capsules, real-time coding, and collaborative projects.

Prerequisites

✔ Basic Python programming (loops, functions, libraries)
✔ Familiarity with Machine Learning fundamentals
✔ Bring your own laptop with Python 3.10+ installed (we'll provide Colab alternatives)

Program Schedule

Hands-on deep dives — from transformers to multi-agent systems.
DAY 1 · May 4 Foundations & Mastering LLMs
09:00 – 10:30

🧠 Theory: Transformers → LLMs

Decoder-only architecture, attention mechanism, context windows, pre-training vs post-training pipelines.

10:30 – 11:30

⚡ Prompt Engineering Masterclass

Few-shot, Chain of Thought (CoT), structured JSON outputs, and programmatic prompting.

11:30 – 12:00

☕ Coffee & Networking

12:00 – 14:00

🛠️ Fine-tuning with PEFT/LoRA

Live workshop: fine-tune Mistral/Llama-3 using Unsloth & Colab, parameter-efficient adaptation.

DAY 2 · May 5 RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
09:00 – 10:30

📚 Theory: RAG pipeline deep dive

Embeddings, vector DBs (Chroma/FAISS), cosine similarity, and evaluation metrics (hallucination).

10:30 – 11:30

🔧 Build your first RAG

Ingest PDFs, chunking, retrieval chain with LangChain, ask questions over private data.

11:30 – 12:00

☕ Coffee Break

12:00 – 14:00

📈 Advanced RAG: hybrid search & RAGAS

Re-ranking, self-query retrieval, and performance evaluation framework.

DAY 3 · May 6 The Era of AI Agents
09:00 – 10:30

🤖 ReAct & Agent Architecture

Reasoning + Acting, agentic workflows, and design patterns for autonomous systems.

10:30 – 11:30

🔌 Tool use & function calling

Implement tools (calculators, APIs) and connect LLMs to external actions.

11:30 – 12:00

☕ Coffee Break

12:00 – 14:00

🚀 Final Project: Multi-Agent Collaboration

Build a team of specialized agents using LangGraph or CrewAI, solve real-world tasks.
